Selena Quintanilla Perez

The Queen of Tejano Music


A timeline of Selena Quintanilla's life:

  • 1971 - Born on April 16th in Lake Jackson Texas, U.S.

  • 1981 - Her father formed a band 'Selena y Los Dinos' with her and her siblings

  • 1984 - She recorded her first LP record "Selena y Los Dinos'

  • 1987 - Won 'Female Vocalist of the Year' award at the Tejano Music Awards

  • 1990 - Selena released her second studio album 'Ven Comigo' where the song 'Baila Esta Cumbia' became one of her most popular songs

  • 1992 - Married her guitarist Chris Perez

  • 1993 - Released her album 'Live!'

  • 1994 - Started her own clothing line
    -Won a Grammy Award for 'Best Mexican/American Album' for Live!

  • 1995 - March 31st dies of a gun shot wound she was 23 years old
    -Her album 'Dreaming of You' was released and started a record by selling 175,000 on release day
    -Selena was inducted into the Billboard Latin Music Hall of Fame, Hard Rock Cafe's Hall of Fame, and the South Texas Hall of Fame.
